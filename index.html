Sample 1 — Personal Goal Statement (SMART)

Goal Summary

Specific: Improve my overall physical fitness and weight reduction from 193lbs to 186lbs by regular exercise and balanced diet.

Measurable: Lose 7lbs and run 3 miles (5 km) without stopping. Track via Samsung Health and Home Workout apps paired with my smartwatch.

Time-bound: 3 months.

Action Plan

Exercise Routine
  1. Weeks 1-4: 30 min cardio + 20 min strength — 3×/week.
  2. Weeks 5-8: 45 min cardio + 30 min strength — 4×/week.
  3. Weeks 9-12: 60 min cardio + 40 min strength — 5×/week.
Diet Plan & Monitoring
  • Increase fruits, vegetables, lean protein, whole grains; reduce processed foods.
  • Track weekly weight & running distance; monthly evaluations and adjustments.
Potential Challenges & Strategies
  • Motivation — set small milestones, rewards, or find a workout buddy.
  • Time constraints — schedule workouts and meal prep.
  • Dietary temptations — plan meals and allow occasional treats.
  • Interruptions — triage family/work tasks quickly to prevent procrastination escalation.

Reflection: Achieving this goal will improve health, energy, and discipline — traits that carry into professional life.

Sample 2 — SMART Goal: Time Management for an IT Supervisor

Specific: Improve time management to oversee team projects, handle administrative tasks, and provide timely staff support.

Measurable: Use Jira/Trello/Asana and complete ≥90% of planned weekly tasks while reducing after-hours work by 50% in 2 months.

Achievable: Block 30 minutes each morning to review priorities and delegate at least one low-priority task weekly.

Time-Bound: 60-day trial with self-review and team feedback.
